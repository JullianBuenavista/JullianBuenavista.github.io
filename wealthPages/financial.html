Financial Wealth

Financial wealth is achieved when:

  • their income covers living expenses and luxuries
  • a person has a buffer for emergency expenses
  • a person has investments that generate passive income

Financial wealth can be achieved by:

  • investing early
  • creating multiple income streams
  • living below their means

Things to avoid:

  • accumulating debt
  • living beyond their means
  • not saving for emergencies
