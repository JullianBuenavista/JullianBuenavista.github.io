Time Wealth

Time wealth is achieved when:

  • you have the freedom to choose how to spend your time
  • you have agency over your schedule
  • you have the ability to prioritize what truly matters

Time wealth can be achieved by:

  • investing early
  • creating multiple income streams
  • living below their means

Things to avoid:

  • procrastination
  • overcommitting to obligations
  • neglecting self-care and rest
